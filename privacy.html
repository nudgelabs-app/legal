SIXMAP

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 8, 2026 · Last updated: May 8, 2026

SIXMAP is an interactive map and progression companion for an open-world game, operated by Nudge-labs.app ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information SIXMAP collects, how it is used, who it is shared with, and the choices you have. SIXMAP is provided as a free Android app via Google Play.

Summary in plain English. SIXMAP does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or real-world location. You sign in anonymously. We collect a username you choose, your in-app activity (places you find, posts you create), and standard mobile diagnostics (crashes, app analytics, advertising identifier). We use Firebase, Supabase, and AppLovin MAX to operate the app and show ads.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Username — chosen by you during onboarding. Visible to other users on the community feed.
  • Home district — an in-app district you select in Settings. Used only to personalize the in-app map experience.
  • Community feed posts and tips — content you submit, including discoveries, intel, and confirmations.

1.2 Information generated by your use of the app

  • In-app progress — markers marked as found, missions completed, REP earned, streak count, saved posts, post confirmations.
  • App preferences — notification toggles, screen-on toggle, theme.
  • Anonymous user identifier — a Firebase-generated user ID. This identifier is not linked to your real identity.

1.3 Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical information — device model, operating system version, app version, language, time zone, crash logs.
  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging token used to deliver notifications you have opted in to.
  • App analytics events — screen views, button taps, session length, feature usage. Used to improve the app.
  • Advertising identifier — the Google Advertising ID provided by your device, used by ad partners to show ads. You can reset or limit this in your Android settings.

1.4 Information we do NOT collect

  • Your real name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • Your real-world geographic location (GPS, Wi-Fi, or cell). The "map" inside SIXMAP is a fictional in-app world, not the real world.
  • Your photos, contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera.
  • Payment information. SIXMAP does not process payments at this time.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core app features — show your map progress, REP, streaks, and feed activity.
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (streak reminders, weekly briefings, tip canonization alerts).
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To understand how the app is used in aggregate, so we can improve it.
  • To show advertisements that help keep SIXMAP free.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.

4. Data Retention

Your in-app progress and content are retained for as long as your anonymous user ID remains active. Because SIXMAP uses anonymous authentication tied to your device, uninstalling the app or clearing app data may sever the link to your progress. Crash logs are retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days. Analytics events are retained per Firebase Analytics defaults.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Push notifications. You can disable specific notification categories in Settings, or revoke notification permission entirely from Android system settings.
  • Advertising ID. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings.
  • Data deletion. Because SIXMAP does not collect identifying information, we cannot link a deletion request to a specific person without your anonymous user ID. To request deletion of your in-app data, email nudgelabs.app@gmail.com with your username and we will locate and remove the associated record.
  • Access requests. Email us at the address above and we will provide the data we hold against your username.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR including the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your personal data, and to object to processing. If you are in India, you have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email above.

6. Children's Privacy

SIXMAP is rated for users aged 17 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

SIXMAP is operated from India. Your data may be processed on servers located in the United States, European Union, or other regions where our service providers operate. By using SIXMAP, you consent to this transfer.

8. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including TLS encryption in transit and access controls on our backend. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
